MyAdventureGame
Have a player go through a series of monsters to prove that they can become a hero
Player's HP: 100 Monster's HP: 50
HP will not reset everytime a new monster is encountered
5 monsters to beat
Everytime a monster is killed, give the user an option to choose a buff.
Buffs: increase HP; increase maxDamage; obtain armour (decreases the monster's maxDamage)
If you dodge successfully half your damage
If the user enters anything else besides the options given, the user loses their chance on whatever the options are.
